About SkyTech Solutions

Pioneering aviation technology solutions since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by aviation industry veterans, SkyTech Solutions was born out of a shared frustration with outdated technology systems in the airline industry.

2010 - The Beginning

Three airline operations experts and two software engineers came together to build better solutions for the industry they loved.

2014 - First Major Client

Implemented our flight operations platform for a regional carrier, proving our technology at scale.

2018 - Global Expansion

Expanded operations to Europe and Asia, serving international airlines with localized solutions.

Today - Industry Leader

Serving over 50 airlines worldwide with a comprehensive suite of aviation technology solutions.
